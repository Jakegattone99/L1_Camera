In this folder, there is a wiring diagram of the system, and a circuit diagram (image & KiCad file). 

Wiring diagram:
  Red wire      =   + battery
  Green wire    =   - battery
  Purple wire   =   hall effect signal
  blue wire     =   buzzer signal

Circuit diagram:
  The circuit diagram goes into more detail of the board, showing the diodes, the resistor, the buzzer, 
  the connectors, and the hall effect sensor.
